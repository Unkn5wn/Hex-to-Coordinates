Hex Coordinate → Latitude/Longitude

Enter your 4-digit hex coordinate (with or without 0x):
  • 1st digit: latitude direction (1=N, 3=S, 4=W)
  • 2nd digit: 1st digit of latitude (A=1, B=2, ... F=6)
  • 3rd digit: 2nd digit of latitude (A=1, B=2, ... F=6)
Longitude is always 24° E.
Example: 0x149C → Latitude: 49° N, Longitude: 24° E
